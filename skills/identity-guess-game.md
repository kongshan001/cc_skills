## 技能描述

身份猜猜猜 - 多人互动推理游戏。每位玩家被分配一个秘密身份，通过3轮线索描述暗示自己的身份，最终猜测其他玩家的身份。

Identity Guess Game - Multiplayer interactive推理 game. Each player is assigned a secret identity, describes clues to hint at their identity through 3 rounds, and ultimately guesses other players' identities.

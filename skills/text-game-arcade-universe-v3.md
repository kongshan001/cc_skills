## 技能描述

综合性 ASCII 文字游戏大厅。严格按规则渲染和裁判五子棋、围棋、井字棋、黑白棋、四子棋、海战棋、中国象棋、国际象棋、扫雷、数独、2048、推箱子、文字迷宫，以及接龙、猜词、猜数、Lights Out、Nim、汉诺塔等小游戏；带强触发词、统一大厅、标准棋盘模板、合法性校验、难度、悔棋与裁判模式。

Comprehensive ASCII text game hall. Strictly render and judge: Gomoku, Go, Tic-Tac-Toe, Reversi, Connect Four, Battleship, Chinese Chess, International Chess, Minesweeper, Sudoku, 2048, Sokoban, Text Maze, plus Solitaire, Word Guess, Number Guess, Lights Out, Nim, Tower of Hanoi and other mini games; with strong trigger words, unified hall, standard board templates, legality verification, difficulty, undo and referee mode.
